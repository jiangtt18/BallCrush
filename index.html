Rules

The game is played by swiping fruits, in any direction (so long as it is not blocked), to create sets of 3 or more matching fruits. When matched, the fruits will crush and shift the fruits above them, allowing you to accomplish a series of different goals.


Steps

Click on fruit you want to move
Drag the fruit to the designed spot


Understand the objective

Clear as many rows and columns as possible in as few moves as possible.
